Both of LaserGRBL and LightBurn are the popular laser control softwares for Computers.

LaserGRBL is open source and free, but only support Windows computers.
LightBurn is a paid software, but could support all of Winows, MAC OS and Linux computers.

You could choose one of them according to your preference.
